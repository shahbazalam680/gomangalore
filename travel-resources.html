Finding Cheap Flights to Mangalore

Air India

Air India is the official carrier of India and they operate direct flights from various destinations in India directly to Mangalore or via Bangalore.

Indigo

Indigo is a domestic airline carrier which flies directly to Mangalore from various destinations in India with some of the cheapest fares.

Skyscanner

Use Skyscanner to compare flight prices and get the best deals. However, after comparing flight prices, I would recommend to book flights via the carrier’s website directly.
